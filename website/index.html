Thesis

Bookmarks are pointers. FieldTheory turns them into memory.

Browser sync keeps a copy. It does not structure retrieval or put the archive under your control.

Replaces

Hosted sync dependence, history drift, and the quiet rot of tab sprawl.

Keeps

Chrome, Brave, local files, and the shell you already use.

Promise

Fast recall for the links you already know are worth keeping.
